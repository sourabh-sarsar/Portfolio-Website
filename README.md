# Sourabh Sarsar — Product Design Portfolio

This portfolio is built to tell the story of a senior product designer who builds human-first digital experiences with a strong focus on AI, SaaS, automotive HMI, and modern web products.

## What this portfolio communicates

- A confident product design narrative that opens with a bold visual identity and craft-first voice.
- A showcase of design thinking through selected case studies, experience highlights, skills, and contact touchpoints.
- A visual system that reflects strong product sensibilities: bold typography, motion-led interactions, and clean information hierarchy.
- An emphasis on outcomes, process, collaboration, and leadership rather than technical implementation details.

## Design intent and experience

### Hero experience

- A headline-first introduction that positions the role clearly: `AI-PRODUCT DESIGNER`.
- A curated first impression using micro animations, motion, layered typography, and branded accent colors to communicate energy and technical confidence.
- A floating profile and stack presentation that reinforce personal brand while giving hints of craft and polish.

### About section

- Describes the designer's core strengths: discovery, research, workshops, wireframing, prototyping, usability testing, and experience optimization.
- Highlights real impact: 8.5 years of design experience across SaaS, AI/ML, automotive, and IT platforms.
- Uses expressive layout and microcopy to communicate professional breadth and product discipline.

### Work section

- Presents handpicked case studies from Behance with strong visual cards and contextual storytelling.
- Supports each project with role-specific details, domain tags, and signal metrics like appreciations and views.
- Positions the work as thoughtful studies rather than a generic gallery.

### Experience section

- Visualizes career progression with an animated timeline and role-based storytelling.
- Communicates product leadership, cross-functional collaboration, and measurable design outcomes.
- Uses distinct UI cues and motion to guide attention through key career moments.

### Skills section

- Organizes capabilities into meaningful categories: Lead, Craft, Tools, AI / Vibe-coding.
- Shows both design leadership and tool fluency, including modern AI-assisted workflows.
- Uses interactive chip styling to reinforce a polished experience.

### Contact and brand section

- Ends with a strong call-to-action: “Let’s build something meaningful.”
- Presents direct contact details, social links, and resume access with high-contrast visual styling.
- Reinforces brand consistency through stacked identity marks and a closing signature.

## Product and UX choices

- Single-page, scroll-driven layout to keep the narrative focused and easy to scan.
- Responsive design for desktop and mobile, with navigation and interaction patterns tailored for both.
- Balanced use of motion and typography to create a premium digital portfolio feel.
- Section-based structure that mirrors a product discovery flow: introduction → context → work → experience → skills → contact.

## Technology at a glance

This portfolio is implemented with a modern front-end stack to support fast performance and polished interactions.

- React 18
- Vite
- Tailwind CSS
- Motion-based animation
- Custom component-driven UI

> The technology is intentionally presented as a supporting layer — the real value is in the experience, portfolio storytelling, and product design craft.

## Component structure overview

- `src/App.jsx` — page layout and section order
- `src/components/Hero.jsx` — lead brand story and hero interaction
- `src/components/About.jsx` — positioning, summary, and craft overview
- `src/components/Work.jsx` — selected case studies and portfolio links
- `src/components/Experience.jsx` — career timeline and impact narrative
- `src/components/SkillsStrip.jsx` — skills, tools, and modern AI tooling
- `src/components/Contact.jsx` — final call-to-action and contact details
- `src/components/Navbar.jsx` — navigation, resume access, and quick connect
- `src/components/ui/Gravity.jsx` — custom interactive visual detail

## Why this portfolio works

- It leads with high-level positioning and then layers in evidence through projects and roles.
- It makes a strong visual statement while remaining functional and easy to navigate.
- It balances brand personality with trust signals and contact availability.
- It is designed to feel like a product experience, not just a static resume.

## Notes for reviewers

- This repository is intended to back a deployed portfolio website and is not focused on local installation instructions.
- The code is structured to support future updates, additional case studies, and more interactive storytelling.
- The design is ready to be deployed as a polished personal brand presence.
